Annual Events

Balloon Festival

  • Ballooning requires very light winds and an absence of thermal activity to be safe and enjoyable, therefore, most balloons fly in the early morning and late evening hours.
  • Please plan to come early in the morning and/or stay after the airshow so you can see the splendor of many colorful balloons inflating and lifting slowly into the sky.
  • Throughout the day we will make announcements to help keep you informed about the timing of balloon events.
  • Many of the balloon pilots will be giving tethered and/or chartered flights. However, the balloon rides are not sold by the Flying Circus. The arrangements for a flight must be made with the balloon pilot and not the Gift Shop attendant.
  • Announcements will be made regarding any space available for passengers.
  • Balloon rides are subject to availability, and existing weather conditions.
  • Tethered BALLOON RIDES are $20/adult and $10/kids (12 and under).

Wing and Wheels

The Flying Circus Airshow in Bealeton performs every Sunday at 2:30 pm from May thru October. Enjoy the greatest All-American Barnstorming Airshow and meet the greatest Car and Motorcycle owners around! The Flying Circus has arranged these special shows just for you:
  • Vehicle registration from 11 am to 2 pm. Airshow from 2:30 to 4:30 pm.
  • Voting from People's Choice ballots, Winners announced at 4:30 pm.
  • Grand prize winner receives the Flying Circus Trophy.
  • This is a rare photo opportunity for you to pose with your wheels and an antique biplane. Bring your camera!
