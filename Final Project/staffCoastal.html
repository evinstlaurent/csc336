Jonathan G. Sabourin, DPM, FACFAS
Foot and Ankle Physician

Dr. Jonathan Sabourin is one of New Englandâ€™s top surgeons for ankle scopes and Brostrom procedures. Graduating from Ohio College of Podiatric Medicine, and completing his Surgical Residency at MetroWest Medical Center specializing in trauma of lower extremity, Dr. Sabourin has a true appreciation for the repair of sports injuries.

He has shared his expertise professionally for almost 20 years by teaching residents and other physicians his procedures. He is a member of the resident selection committee for Charter Care and Roger Williams Medical Center. Dr. Sabourin is also a surgical instructor for ankle arthroscopy and surgical techniques.

He is a board member of the American College of Foot and Ankle Surgeons, board qualified in Reconstructive Foot and Ankle Surgery by the American College of Foot and Ankle Surgeons, and board qualified by the American Board of Podiatric Orthopedics and Primary Podiatric Medicine.

Currently, Dr. Sabourin is the President and a board member of the Rhode Island Podiatric Medical Association. Dr. Sabourin is a Southcoast affiliate specialist for Charlton Memorial Hospital, a Lifespan affiliate specialist for The Miriam Hospital and Newport Hospital, and has privileges at Roger Williams Hospital.
