New Patient Information

Interested In Coastal Foot and Ankle?

If you are looking for a specialized podiatry practice with personalized care to treat your lower-extremity issue, Coastal Foot and Ankle is here to help you and we are always accepting new patients! If our practice seems like a good fit for you, give us a call to schedule an initial appointment today. We look forward to hearing from you!

You can dowload our new patient inkake forms to the right or linked here and bring them completed to your appointment.

Expectations for Your First Visit!

Our office will give you a call roughly 48 hours before your scheduled appointment to confirm the date and time and ensure you are prepared for your first Coastal Foot and Ankle consultation.

All patients at Coastal Foot and Ankle are required to have updated patient intake forms on file with us. For new patients, please arrive 30 minutes early to the office to fill out these forms. If you care to fill the forms out prior to your appointment, a PDF link can be found at the bottom of this page stating "Click Here to Download Patient Intake Forms". If you have your paperwork completed prior to your arrival, please arrive 15 minutes early for your visit.

What To Bring to Your Initial Appointment:

  • Photo I.D.
    • Driver's License
    • State-issued I.D.
    • Military I.D.
    • Federal-issued I.D.
    • Passport
  • Valid Primary and Secondary Insurance Cards
  • List of Current Medications and Allergies
  • Completed New Patient Intake Forms
