Welcome to Coastal Foot and Ankle:

Our Mission

The pride of New England is their coasts, so at Coastal Foot and Ankle, we pride ourselves in our committed leading-edge surgical and nonsurgical podiatric care and catering atmosphere. Highly trained board-certified medical professionals and exemplary, caring staff provide the best podiatric service for all patients. Our business model is all about a team mentality of working together to find the best solution for all concerns and finding the best treatment options for a speedy recovery. We are specialized in handling simple podiatric needs to surgical repair. Nothing but respect and kindness will be shown in our offices. Come see us today!

Coastal Foot and Ankle in the Community!

Listen to Dr. Jonathan Sabourin and Stephanie on Elder Affairs!

Every 3rd Friday of the month at 12 PM, tune in to WADK Newport 1540 AM to hear our very own Dr. Jonathan Sabourin and practice manager Stephanie sit down with Jane Roggero for the Elder Affairs podcast discussing issues impacting our local senior community!

Getting Hands On with Dr. Jonathan Sabourin!

On July 11, 2022, Dr. Sabourin instructed at a surgical cadaver lab at the Arthrex Cadaveric Lab in Westboro, MA, demonstrating and teaching different podiatric surgeries and surgical techniques to Kent County Hospital residents.

Dr. Jonathan Sabourin: President!

On September 29, 2022, Dr. Sabourin was inaugurated as the President of the Rhode Island Podiatric Medical Association (RIPMA). Dr. Sabourin had previously served as RIPMA's Vice President. He is pictured here after the ceremony with his podiatry residents!

Giving Back with Coastal Foot and Ankle!

On December 10, 2022, Coastal Foot and Ankle was a proud sponsor of the Stuff-A-Sprinter Toy Drive with Viti Tiverton and WADK Newport to benefit the MLK Community Center in Newport, RI. Happy Holidays!
